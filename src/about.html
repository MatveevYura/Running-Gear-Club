About Us

LAUNCHED IN 2021

We've been building platforms for runners since 2020. We have been working with and for runners for the past two years and knew that one of their biggest pain points is how expensive the sport can be when you do it regularly.

That is why we created Running Gear Club - a community where we can use the power of the group to negotiate exclusive discounts, freebies, competitions and more for our members.

The more members we have, the bigger the discounts we can secure! So tell your friends to join!
