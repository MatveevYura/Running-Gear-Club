About Runners Need

Whilst it may look like a fresh modern online running gear store, Runners Need has actually be around since 1982 when founder Chris Bould setup the first store in Camden, London. Since then the brand has continued to grow and alongside the website you can also visit one of their 40 stores dotted around the UK.

Runners Need has one of the most complete catalogues of products for runners with everything from running shoes, apparel, accessories and recovery equipment available to buy today. As runners it is always great to find a store that will allow you to stock on everything in one place rather than having to buy items across a number of stores and Runners Need is certainly up there as one of the most complete stores online for runners.

Our expert tip for shopping with Runners Need is to subscribe to their email newsletter, they do not spam you with emails and you can be sure you will never miss out on a new product launch every again and can be wearing the latest running shoes before your friends even realize they are available for sale.
